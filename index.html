Our Story

Sanoor Cashews began in 1982 in the small village of Sanoor, nestled between Udupi and Mangalore in Karnataka. Founded by Late Mr. Ganesh Kamath — a Mechanical Engineering graduate from MIT Manipal — what started as a humble startup has grown into a legacy of premium quality cashews trusted across generations.

Our Leadership

Late Mr. Ganesh Kamath

Founder & Managing Director

A Mechanical Engineering graduate from MIT Manipal, Mr. Ganesh Kamath founded Sanoor Cashews in 1982 with a vision to bring premium quality cashews from the village of Sanoor to the world. His 42 years of industry expertise laid the foundation of this legacy.

Shuklambar Prabhu

Director — Operations & Business Development

A Mechanical Engineering graduate from Hirasugar Institute of Technology with 6 years experience at L&T Mumbai and KPIT Cummins Pune. Returned to lead the family business alongside his wife Ashwini Kamath.

Ashwini Kamath / Swathi Prabhu
